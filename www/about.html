Personal Finance Tracker

Personal Finance Tracker is a lightweight Shiny application that helps you turn raw bank statements into clear, actionable insights. Upload a PDF, assign or tweak spending categories on the fly, visualise where the money goes, and get an instant 50-30-20 budget suggestion – all in one place and without leaving your browser.

What the app does

  • Import statements (PDF) – automatic text extraction & parsing into tidy tables.
  • Smart categorisation – rules catch common merchants, you can edit each row with a dropdown and save.
  • Interactive analysis – bar chart of total outflows by category, date-level trend plot, and a quick pie overview.
  • Budget calculator
  • Export – download your cleaned data as CSV for further analysis or archiving.

Supported banks / PDF formats

The current parser recognises and processes statements from:

  • Santander Bank Polska
  • Bank Millennium
  • Citi Handlowy (Citi Bank PL credit-card statement)
  • Revolut (Polish zloty statement)
  • PKO Bank Polski

How to use

  1. Go to the Transactions tab  →  click Upload statement and select a PDF.
  2. Drag the date-range filter or edit categories via the dropdowns.
  3. Optionally add cash transactions manually with the form on the left.
  4. Explore Spending Analysis and Summary tabs for visuals. 
  5. Open Budget Calculator, enter your monthly net income and compare with the recommended split.

Data privacy

All parsing is done in-memory inside your browser session on the server. Uploaded PDFs are not stored permanently; they’re discarded when the session ends. You can always download the processed CSV and delete it locally if needed.
